# 7‑Layer ERP / Software Selection Methodology  
*Based on field research in 80 FMCG distribution companies (developed 13 years ago)*

---

## Layer 1 — Planning & Roadmap Definition  
- Define project scope, objectives, and justification  
- Align expectations across departments (Finance, HR, IT, Production, Supply Chain)  
- Identify technical and organizational constraints  
- Create project timeline (Gantt) and obtain approval  
- Estimate preliminary budget  

---

## Layer 2 — Technical & Functional Requirements Analysis  
- Conduct interviews with department managers  
- Document technical requirements (OS, DB, integration, performance, security)  
- Document functional requirements for each subsystem  
- Prioritize requirements (Must‑Have / Should‑Have / Nice‑to‑Have)  
- Define evaluation KPIs  

---

## Layer 3 — Market Research & Vendor List Creation  
- Identify ERP/software vendors active in the FMCG distribution sector  
- Collect product information, technology stack, and references  
- Filter vendors based on technical fit, industry fit, and scalability  
- Prepare initial long‑list  

---

## Layer 4 — Software Demonstrations (Demos)  
- Provide vendors with demo scenarios  
- Evaluate usability, workflow coverage, reporting, integration, and flexibility  
- Score each demo based on predefined KPIs  
- Document strengths and weaknesses  

---

## Layer 5 — Comparison & Decision Making  
- Build comparison matrix (technical + functional + financial)  
- Weight criteria based on business priorities  
- Compare shortlisted vendors  
- Conduct risk assessment  
- Select top candidates  

---

## Layer 6 — Negotiation & Contracting  
- Negotiate pricing, licensing, implementation scope, SLAs  
- Validate vendor commitments  
- Finalize contract terms  
- Approve implementation plan  

---

## Layer 7 — Implementation & Rollout  
- Prepare detailed implementation roadmap  
- Data migration planning  
- User training  
- Pilot rollout  
- Go‑Live  
- Post‑Go‑Live support and optimization
