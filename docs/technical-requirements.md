# Technical & Functional Requirements  
*Extracted from interviews with Finance, HR, Logistics, Warehouse, Treasury, and IT managers*

---

## 1. Technical Requirements (General)

- Windows Server 2012 R2 (64‑bit) compatibility  
- SQL Server 2012+ support  
- Full access to database tables for IT team  
- Modern development stack (C#, .NET)  
- High performance (millions of transactions)  
- Full subsystem integration  
- Audit trail for all user actions  
- Dynamic reporting (custom fields, formulas)  

---

## 2. Functional Requirements by Subsystem

### **Financial Accounting**
- Multi‑level chart of accounts (Group, GL, Subsidiary, Cost Center, Project)  
- Journal entry management  
- Draft vs. final documents  
- Excel import for vouchers  
- Automatic opening/closing entries  
- Multi‑level detailed reporting  
- Access control and approval workflows  

---

### **Fixed Assets**
- Asset registration with technical attributes  
- Tree‑structured asset classification  
- Depreciation (multiple methods)  
- Asset transfer, disposal, sale  
- Integration with Warehouse, Maintenance, Industrial Accounting  
- Insurance and location tracking  

---

### **Treasury / Cash & Bank**
- Managing cash, bank accounts, petty cash  
- Incoming/outgoing payments (cash, cheque, guarantee, promissory note)  
- Bank reconciliation  
- Cash flow reporting  
- Automated financial postings  
- Cheque lifecycle management  
- Electronic bank statements  

---

### **Payroll & HR**
- Import personnel data, contracts, attendance  
- Insurance & tax file generation  
- Salary calculation (overtime, bonuses, loans, deductions)  
- Multi‑branch payroll posting  
- Custom payslip and HR forms  
- Annual bonus and tax rules  
- Excel import for adjustments  

---

### **Warehouse / Inventory**
- Item cardex (quantity + value)  
- Receipts, issues, returns  
- Price adjustments  
- Contractor consignment tracking  
- Integration with Sales, Purchasing, Production  
- Multi‑decimal pricing  
- Prevent deletion when stock control is active  

---

### **Contracts Accounting**
- Register purchase/sales/service contracts  
- Progress payments  
- Penalties, bonuses  
- Guarantees and extensions  
- Contract reminders  
- Contract performance reporting  

---

### **Industrial / Cost Accounting**
- BOM definition  
- Standard vs. actual production  
- Cost allocation (labor, overhead, depreciation)  
- Machine utilization rates  
- ABC costing  
- Integration with Warehouse, Fixed Assets, Production  

---

### **Foreign Currency Accounting**
- FX accounts  
- FX revaluation  
- Multi‑currency vouchers  
- FX‑based financial reporting  

---

### **Consolidation Accounting**
- Holding structure definition  
- Subsidiary consolidation  
- Intercompany eliminations  
- Unified chart of accounts  
- Consolidated financial statements
