# Vendor Shortlisting Process

---

## 1. Market Research
- Identify ERP vendors active in FMCG distribution  
- Collect product brochures, demos, references  
- Review technology stack and integration capabilities  

---

## 2. Long‑List Creation
- 20–30 vendors initially considered  
- Basic filtering based on:
  - Industry relevance  
  - Technical compatibility  
  - Company size  
  - Support availability  

---

## 3. Short‑List Criteria
- Must meet essential technical requirements  
- Must cover core FMCG distribution workflows  
- Must support financial, warehouse, HR, and treasury modules  
- Must have successful implementations in similar companies  

---

## 4. Short‑List Output
Typically 3–5 vendors remain after filtering.

---

## 5. Reasons for Vendor Elimination
- Outdated technology  
- Weak reporting  
- No integration capability  
- No FMCG distribution experience  
- High cost  
- Poor demo performance
